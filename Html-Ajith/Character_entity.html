Character entities are used to display reserved characters in HTML.

A character entity looks like:

  • &entity_name;

    • or

  • &#entity_number;

    • Some Examples are:

      4 < 5

      6 > 4

      Cat & Dog are domestic animals

      Hello "Good morning"

      hello 'Good Morning'

      The symbol of currency pound is £

      The symbol of currency Euro is €
